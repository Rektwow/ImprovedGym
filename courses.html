Group Fitness Schedule

-Thai Fit-

Thai Fit offers you a complete and meaningful workout that promotes muscle building and fat burning. it's a perfect mixture between various elements from aerobics, conditioning and martial arts.

-Box & Kick-

Get ready for a challenging boxing or Kicking work out. We coach you through 7 rounds of bag work that will improve your speed, power and endurance that leave you dripping in sweat.

-Hiit-

Push yourself as you rotate rapidly through multiple circuits using strength, conditioning and agility exercises to burn maximum calories.

-Total Core-

A brutal workout that will squeeze everything out of you, physically and mentally.
7 Exercises, 7 Sets, 7 Repetition, 70s rest between Sets.
